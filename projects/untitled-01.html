Untitled-01 — Simone Grimaldi (2024).

Sculptural work composed of three untreated iron sheets. The visible “mark” on each surface is not applied manually, but generated through the corrosive action of atmospheric agents on the exposed sections of the material. The work explores oxidation as a natural drawing process, allowing time, weather, and matter to define the final image. Single plate: 25 × 50 × 0.25 cm.
