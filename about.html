Who am I?

Hi, I’m Simone, a Neapolitan graphic designer and artist based in Milan.

My practice is rooted in material and manual processes, which I use as the starting point of my creative and design methodology. Most of what I produce comes from observing reality and reinterpreting it through graphic design, visual language, and image-making.

I approach projects with a creative and problem-solving mindset. I believe that every challenge has a solution, and being aware of that is an essential part of my way of working.

Alongside graphic design, I have a strong interest in contemporary art, music, and cultural event organization. These fields constantly feed into my visual research and shape the way I build concepts, identities, and narratives.
